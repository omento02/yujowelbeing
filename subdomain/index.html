Inspiration Behind This Project:

Working in busy hospitals, I saw firsthand the challenges that healthcare providers face, especially when time is crucial. With patients waiting in long queues, every moment counts. Yet, navigating the Standard Treatment Guidelines to find the correct protocol for each case was often time-consuming and cumbersome.

We waited for PDF search results to load, only to scroll through several unrelated sections, hunting down the right page to confirm treatment for each condition. It was frustrating to see valuable time slip away while patients waited.

This project was born out of that necessity. By organizing the document for faster navigation, I hope to ease some of the stress for healthcare professionals and contribute to a more efficient, compassionate patient experience.
